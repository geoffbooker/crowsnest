About The Crow's Nest

A unique freehouse bar in Sutton-on-Sea with a friendly, relaxed and dog friendly atmosphere we offer a mouth watering selection of cask ales, wines and spirits including an extensive gin menu and tonic menu.
We also provide an unbeatable selection of teas and coffees.
